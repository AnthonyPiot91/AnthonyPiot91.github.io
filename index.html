I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Integrative and System Biology (IBIS) at Laval University, QC, Canada.
I am interested in studying the evolution of organisms using genomics and molecular approaches. To this end, I take advantage of bioinformatics to manipulate the large amount of molecular data produced by high throughput sequencing technologies.
